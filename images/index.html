About Island Party

Since the foundation of BYX at Texas A&M in 1994, Beta Upsilon Chi Fraternity has hosted Island Party as our own way of giving back to the community. Although Island Party has grown and adapted over the years, its purpose remains the same: evangelism, philanthropy, fun, life-giving, and the bolstering of our own brotherhood and unity. This year we would like to invite you to Island Party 2017. Join us for a laid-back, family-friendly atmosphere to boot.
